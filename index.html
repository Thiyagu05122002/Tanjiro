Tanjiro Kamado

Harshira! Sun Breathing User.

He is a Demon Slayer in the Demon Slayer Corps, who joined to find a remedy to turn his sister, Nezuko Kamado, back into a human and to hunt down and kill demons

Tanjiro's Sister

  • Her name is Nezuko kamado
  • Nezuko is a petite young girl with fair skin, visibly large, prominent fangs, and sharp, stiletto nails with a base color of light pink and ombre fading to a red-pinkish color at the end where they point.
